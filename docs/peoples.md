Mr. Venkateswara Babu Kanneganti Director Gourmet Popcornica Pvt ltd

Mr. Venkateswara Babu Kanneganti brings over 30 years of expertise in large-scale manufacturing and project leadership. He currently oversees the Oil Palm Division at Gourmet Popcornica, driving growth and innovation across strategic initiatives.

He is the Founder Director of Rankars BayGroup, one of India’s largest salt manufacturing and export companies, where he established Gujarat’s first indigenous salt washery to meet international standards. He also pioneered the processing and export of Senna Leaf from Gujarat and Rajasthan to European markets, opening new trade corridors forIndian agriculture.

Beyond manufacturing, Mr. Kanneganti co-developed and patented IcedCAD software in partnership with an American company, showcasing his ability to merge industry with technology. His entrepreneurial vision further extended into renewable energy, where he successfully commissioned a 7.5 MW waste-to-energy project, underscoring his commitment to sustainability.

With a career spanning manufacturing, technology, and renewable energy, Mr. Kanneganti exemplifies leadership that transforms industries and sets benchmarks for excellence.
