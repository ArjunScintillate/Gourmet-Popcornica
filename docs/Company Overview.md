Gourmet Popcornica Private Limited – Company Overview
Gourmet Popcornica Private Limited is India’s leading premium popcorn brand, known for
transforming popcorn from a commodity snack into a gourmet, experiential product.
Founded in 2014 in Chennai, India, the company was created with a simple but ambitious
vision: to elevate the cinema and snacking experience through high-quality ingredients,
innovative flavours, and consistent product excellence, while ensuring that farmers are
central to the growth story.
The journey of Gourmet Popcornica started with a strategic partnership with Preferred
Popcorn, USA, combining world-class farming expertise and kernel quality with local
cultivation and processing. This collaboration has enabled the delivery of premium popcorn
products to Indian cinemas and retail, while steadily building self-sustaining operations that
uphold international standards.
Over the past decade, Gourmet Popcornica has emerged as a category creator in the Indian
popcorn market, setting new benchmarks in taste, quality, and scale.
What the Company Does
Gourmet Popcornica specialises in the growing, sourcing, processing, and distribution of
premium popcorn kernels and ready-to-cook popcorn products. The company works closely
with Indian farmers across nine states in India to cultivate popcorn maize suited to Indian
agro-climatic conditions, reducing dependence on imports and building a sustainable
domestic supply chain. Today, Gourmet Popcornica has over 36,000 acres under cultivation,
working with 17,500 farmers.
Its portfolio spans:
 Premium popcorn kernels
 Cinema and institutional popcorn solutions
 Ready-to-cook and flavoured popcorn offerings
This has resulted in delicious products, with customised recipes, and everything in between
– Gourmet Popcornica is the go-to for all popcorn needs. Available at over 3,400 cinemas
across India, Gourmet Popcornica is the preferred supplier to India’s largest cinema chains,
offering customised flavours and blends. Premixed popcorn flavours and seasonings are
developed by the in-house team, in accordance with customer preferences.
The brand is particularly well known for its strong presence in cinemas, multiplexes, and
organised food service.
Market Leadership &amp; Scale
Gourmet Popcornica is widely recognised as a market leader in the premium popcorn
segment in India. Its products are used by a significant share of organised cinema chains
and food service operators across the country. The company’s scale, quality control, and
consistency have made it a trusted partner for institutional clients and a preferred brand for
consumers seeking a superior popcorn experience.
Farmer Partnerships &amp; Indigenous Innovation
A defining aspect of Gourmet Popcornica’s journey has been its focus on building India’s
popcorn maize ecosystem. Through structured farmer engagement that spans over 40,000
acres across nine states in India, agronomic support, and assured procurement, the
company has played a catalytic role in encouraging Indian farmers to adopt popcorn maize
as a viable crop.

This farm-to-fork approach supports rural livelihoods while strengthening India’s self-reliance
in a niche but growing agricultural segment.
Quality, R&amp;D and Sustainability
Quality assurance and innovation are central to Gourmet Popcornica’s operations. The
company invests in research, testing, and process improvement to ensure consistency in
kernel expansion, taste, and performance—critical parameters for cinema and food service
partners.
Sustainability is embedded across the value chain, from responsible sourcing and efficient
processing to waste minimisation and long-term farmer partnerships.
Brand Philosophy
Positioned around the idea of Everything Popcorn… Everything Good, Gourmet Popcornica
combines indulgence with responsibility – bringing together flavour innovation, operational
excellence, and inclusive growth.
Looking Ahead
As India’s snacking and entertainment consumption continues to grow, Gourmet Popcornica
is focused on deepening its leadership in popcorn, expanding consumer touchpoints, and
strengthening its contribution to India’s agri-food ecosystem.
