1. Company Overview

About Us

Founded with a vision to deliver world-class popcorn experiences through consistency, quality, and scale, Gourmet Popcornica is the 5th largest producer of popcorn in the world.

What began as a focused effort to improve popcorn quality in India has evolved into a growing ecosystem serving cinemas, institutional partners, and food businesses across the country. Today, Gourmet Popcornica stands at the intersection of agriculture, innovation, and large-scale supply, helping shape the future of popcorn in India.

Our commitment to excellence reaches from the seed in the ground to the final popped kernel. By investing heavily in contract farming and processing infrastructure, we ensure that every kernel meets international gourmet standards, fostering trust at every level of the supply chain.

“Our mission is simple — to create a self-reliant India through innovation, trust, and the power of our farmers.”

2. Founder Profile

The Visionary

Mr. S.B.P. Pattabhi Rama Rao

Founder & Managing Director

Mr. S.B.P. Pattabhi Rama Rao is pioneering the complete popcorn value chain in India. Since incorporating the company in 2014, he has built a farmer-centric powerhouse connecting cultivation and processing to retail and cinemas. At a time when India depended heavily on imports, he envisioned a self-reliant domestic popcorn ecosystem.

Under his leadership, Gourmet Popcornica has grown to deliver international-quality gourmet popcorn to discerning consumers nationwide. With a stellar track record in food & beverage and retail, he previously spearheaded SPI Cinemas' operations and drove the successful launch and expansion of CookieMan, the iconic Australian cookie brand, across India.

Over the last decade, he has scaled the business from a ₹9 Crore operation to a ₹500+ Crore market leader, commanding nearly 48% of the country's popcorn market. Furthermore, his efforts in indigenizing popcorn cultivation have empowered over 17,500 local farmers and established a self-reliant agricultural ecosystem.
