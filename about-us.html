About Gourmet Popcornica

Gourmet Popcornica is the 5th largest producer of popcorn in the world, built with a vision to deliver world-class popcorn experiences through consistency, quality, and scale.

What began as a focused effort to improve popcorn quality in India has evolved into a growing ecosystem serving cinemas, institutional partners, and food businesses across the country.

Today, Gourmet Popcornica stands at the intersection of agriculture, innovation, and large-scale supply, helping shape the future of popcorn in India.

Pattabhi Rama Rao

A Vision That Changed the Industry

At the heart of Gourmet Popcornica is the vision of Pattabhi Rama Rao, a leader who saw the potential to transform India’s popcorn industry.

At a time when India depended on imports, Gourmet Popcornica pioneered a new domestic popcorn ecosystem built for scale.

What began as a pursuit of better quality evolved into a mission, to create a reliable supply chain that connects farmers, technology, and institutions.

Through years of focused effort, this vision has helped shape a growing domestic popcorn industry, positioning India for long-term self-reliance.

From Product to Ecosystem

The journey of Gourmet Popcornica has been driven by one clear philosophy — consistency creates trust.

By focusing on quality, operational reliability, and long-term relationships, the company has steadily expanded its presence across the institutional popcorn industry.

Today, Gourmet Popcornica works with cinema chains, distributors, food businesses, and institutional partners across India, delivering solutions built for consistency and scale.
